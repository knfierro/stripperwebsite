    FAQ

    1Can I add more time once the night has started?

    Yes, unless the dancers are booked for another event that evening. If you are interested in adding on more time, just notify your dancer so she can check with her schedule. Payment can be negotiaited with your dancer if she chooses to stay for another hour and has no other schedule conflicts.

    2 Do I need to tip?

    Although not required, tips are expected and highly appreciated. Tips can be made in the form of cash, Apple pay, Cash app and Venmo depending on the dancer and what platform she uses.

    3When is payment due?

    We require 20% down in order to reserve. Payment will be made in full when the dancers arrive, before they begin the entertainment.
    We accept all major credit cards, cash, Apple Pay, Zelle and Venmo.

If your dancers are arrive and you aren't blown away, you will be fully refunded.
